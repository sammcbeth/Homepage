Multi-Author Blog

Tech Stack:

  • MongoDB Atlas
  • Django
  • Angular
  • Bootstrap

Description

I built this blog in order to better facilitate my use of the Feynman Technique. The process is to take whatever process you are learning for the day, at the end try to explain it as though you are speaking to someone who has very little to no knowledge in the subject. You then identify gaps in your reasoning and iterate this process until it feels concrete. I try to post once a day but have not been exceptionally consistent.

Pharmaceutical Actions Page

Tech Stack:

  • MongoDB Atlas
  • Django
  • Angular
  • Bootstrap

Description

One of my hobbies is investing in the stock market. As of late I began investing in pharmaceutical stocks leading up to actions that I felt would influence their stock price such as getting a novel drug approved by the FDA. I struggled to find one good source of this information and so was having aggregate from multiple sources. I figured if I was going through the process of aggregating anyway it might be useful to have a site to display that info.
